## 🌀 Part 2: The Core Mechanic (0:30 - 1:15)

*(Hold Space, Speak)*: 'Architect, grant me the swiftness of the wind. Double my movement speed.'

Behind the scenes, **ElevenLabs Scribe v2** transcribes my intent. Then, **Mistral devstral-medium** analyzes the current `game_logic.py`, refactors the physics parameters, and generates a valid Python patch.

*(Show the speed increase)*
The engine hot-reloads the logic instantly. No restarts, no pauses. My intent just became reality. This isn't just changing a value; the AI understands the code structure and applies precise logic shifts in microseconds."

## 💡 Quick Tips for the Recording:
1. **Screen Capture**: Use OBS to record the game window.
2. **Audio**: Ensure your mic is clear so Scribe v2 can pick you up well (it's very accurate, but good audio helps the demo feel premium).
3. **Pacing**: Don't be afraid to cut out the few seconds of wait time during the Mistral refactor and narrate over it—it makes the video feel punchier!
