MistralHack is a pioneering voice-controlled RPG where players interact with an omnipresent "Architect" AI to reshape the game's source code in real-time. By leveraging Mistral devstral-medium for live logic patching and ElevenLabs for seamless voice-to-text and narration, we’ve turned the act of coding into a high-stakes "Reality-Shifting" game mechanic. In this generative adventure, players must earn Sigils of Truth, solve hidden puzzles through verbal commands, and face off against Lillith in a battle where your imagination and voice are the only limits. MistralHack bridges the gap between human intent and executable logic, demonstrating the future of low-latency, AI-augmented interactive entertainment.
